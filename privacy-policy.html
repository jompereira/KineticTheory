01

Information we collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide and improve the game experience.

Information you provide

  • Display name and email address, if you sign in with Apple or Google Maze Racer
  • Support messages you send to us directly

Information collected automatically

  • Gameplay data — current level, progress metrics, and other game state, stored locally and synced via Firebase Cloud Save Maze Racer
  • Analytics events — level completions, session starts, ad interactions, and purchase events, via Firebase Analytics Maze Racer
  • Device identifiers — advertising ID (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android), used only for ad personalisation with your consent Maze Racer
  • Crash and performance data — anonymised diagnostic information, collected in both games

Information we do not collect

  • Payment card numbers or banking details (handled entirely by the App Store / Google Play)
  • Precise location data
  • Contacts, camera, or microphone access

02

How we use your data

  • To save and sync your game progress across devices
  • To restore purchases made through the App Store or Google Play
  • To measure feature engagement and improve game balance
  • To serve advertising (see Section 4)
  • To respond to support requests
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

03

Third-party services

Our games integrate the following third-party SDKs. Each operates under its own privacy policy.

Firebase (Google LLC) Maze Racer

Provides authentication, cloud save, and analytics. Data may be transferred to and stored on Google servers. Firebase Privacy Policy

Google AdMob Maze Racer

Serves interstitial and rewarded video ads. May collect advertising identifiers subject to your consent choices. Google Privacy Policy

Unity Technologies

Provides the game engine and in-app purchase infrastructure across all Kinetic Theory titles. Unity Privacy Policy

Apple (Sign in with Apple) Maze Racer

Used for optional account creation on iOS. Apple does not share your Apple ID email with us unless you choose to reveal it. Apple Privacy Policy

Google (Sign in with Google) Maze Racer

Used for optional account creation on Android. Google Privacy Policy

04

Advertising

Maze Racer

Maze Racer is free to play and supported by advertising. We show two types of ads:

  • Interstitial ads — shown automatically between levels
  • Rewarded video ads — optional, watched in exchange for in-game lives

On first launch, we display a consent form compliant with the IAB TCF 2.2 standard and Google's EU User Consent Policy. On iOS, we request App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission before accessing your IDFA. If you decline, only non-personalised ads are shown.

Purchasing the Remove Ads product permanently suppresses all interstitial ads. Rewarded video ads remain available as an optional, player-initiated feature.

Connect the Colors

Connect the Colors does not currently display advertising.

05

In-app purchases

Some Kinetic Theory titles offer optional in-app purchases processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We never see or store your payment information. Purchases are validated against the relevant store on each session start and can be restored at any time using the in-game restore button.

Maze Racer

  • Remove Ads — permanently removes interstitial ads (non-consumable)
  • Life Refill — instantly refills your lives to the maximum (consumable)

Connect the Colors

Connect the Colors does not currently offer in-app purchases.

06

Authentication

Maze Racer

Creating an account is optional. You may play as a guest, in which case your progress is saved locally and anonymously in Firebase under a randomly generated user ID. If you sign in with Apple or Google, your display name and email are associated with your Firebase account solely to identify your cloud save data. We do not share this with third parties.

You can delete your account at any time by contacting us (see Section 11). Deletion removes your Firebase account and all associated cloud data within 30 days.

Connect the Colors

Connect the Colors does not require or offer account creation. No personal data is collected beyond anonymised crash diagnostics.

07

Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. Analytics data is retained in aggregated, anonymised form for up to 14 months per Google's standard retention policy.

If you uninstall a game without deleting your account, your cloud save data remains stored until you request deletion.

08

Children

Kinetic Theory games are not directed at children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Advertising shown to users who have not consented to personalisation is always non-personalised and complies with applicable child-directed advertising regulations.

09

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Withdraw consent (including ad personalisation consent) at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority
To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address in Section 11. We will respond within 30 days.

Changing your ad consent

You can revisit your ad consent choices at any time through the in-game settings menu in Maze Racer. On iOS, you can also manage ATT permission under Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

10

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page and, where feasible, notify you via an in-app notice on first launch after the change.

Continued use of any Kinetic Theory game after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.
